Help & Support
Frequently asked questions
How do I book a service? ▾
Go to your dashboard and tap "Book Service". Select a category, describe the problem, review the estimate, and choose your preferred slot.
What is the visit charge? ▾
A visit charge of ₹100 is applicable for every technician visit. This is paid after the service is completed.
How soon will a technician arrive? ▾
For Emergency bookings, a technician arrives within 20 minutes. For Normal bookings, you choose the date and time.
Can I cancel a booking? ▾
Yes, you can cancel a booking from My Bookings screen before the technician is dispatched.
How do I contact support? ▾
Email us at support@urbanfix.in or call our helpline at 1800-URBAN-FIX (available 24x7).
